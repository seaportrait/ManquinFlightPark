About Us

Manquin Flight Park is a small hang gliding club located east/north east of Richmond Virginia near the Queenfield Golf club in Manquin, Virginia.

Currently, the park only cators to hang glider platform launching via truck towing that is capable of lauching pilots to various altitude pending weather conditions and pilot's skill.

First time pilots flying at the site will be assess to determine weather conditions allowable to fly. Pending availability, the park is only open for flight operations on weekends.

New pilots interested in flying at the park pending assessment, will be allow to fly during the early morning hours or late evening hours depending on weather conditions until clear to fly during the mid-day hours.

While the Manquin Flight Park welcomes all certified hang glider pilots, the flight park has a responsibility to ensure a safety flying environment for guest pilots and members.

All pilots including members are soley responsibile in maintaining their flight skills in accordance with their United States Hang Gliding and Paragliding (USHPA) ratings and the Manquin Flight Park standards.

Because Manquin Flight Park is NOT a flight school, pilots exhibiting flying charactistics that causes safety concerns to the park is soley responsible in taking the neccessary

steps to seek out flight schools that can assist in bringing their flight skills to the appropriate level according to their ratings registered with the USHPG.

Any pilot identified as deficient in their flight handling skills that poses a saftey hazard to themselves and/or to club members will not be allow to fly at the Manquin Flight Park until those deficiencies are resolved.
